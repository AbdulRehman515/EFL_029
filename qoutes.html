Understanding Your Quote

What each section of your investment quote means

Projected Value

Minimum and maximum estimated portfolio value after 1, 5, and 10 years based on plan performance ranges.

Total Fees

Cumulative management fees over the investment period, calculated monthly on your portfolio value.

Total Tax

Estimated tax liability based on profit thresholds and current tax legislation for your chosen plan.

Total Profit

Net profit after deducting all fees and taxes from your investment returns over the specified period.
